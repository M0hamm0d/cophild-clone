Our Philosophy

To be at the cutting edge of technology through the deployment of knowledge and technology know-how for the benefit of our internal and external stakeholders. We have invested time and resources to make sure that our training programs meet three main objectives;

Right Training

We enhance skills transfer through a combination of excellent training and hands-on lab work.

Right Certificate

As an authorised and accredited exam centre in Nigeria, we prepare candidates for their professional exams during their training. With this they achieve a certification that is recognised worldwide.

Right Jobs

We ensure that the right candidate gets the right career opportunity.

Employment Opportunities

Industrial training undergraduates are accepted and given the opportunity to take up any training of their choice at a discounted fee during their 6 months to 1 year training.

National Youth Corp members are also given a discount on a training of their choice when they present us with a valid Identity.

Cophild also engage its students in field work. We expose them to real life scenarios and take them with us whenever there is a field work experience opportunity. We have employed students that have undertaken trainings with us to become instructors in their areas of specialisation.

Cophild always keep a database of their past and current students. With this we keep them posted on activities, employment and field work openings when the opportunity arises.
